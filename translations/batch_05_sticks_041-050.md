### ENGLISH TRANSLATION

Upper Fortune (Good / Auspicious)

**Fortune Poem:**
High in the heavens, a single branch stands tall,
Clearly divining the winds of nine autumns.
Seizing the moment with noble courage, knowing none can rival,
In the palace examination, fame proclaimed as number one.

**Interpretation:**
If you seek fame and fortune, you shall take the leading position. The bright moon in heaven, ten thousand miles meeting autumn.

**Extended Interpretation:**
• This is an exceptionally auspicious fortune predicting first-place success in competitive endeavors. Like topping the imperial examinations, you will emerge as the clear winner in whatever you pursue.
• The timing is perfect for bold action. The imagery of "seizing the moment" suggests that opportunities are ripe and your courage will be rewarded with unmatched success.
• Fame and recognition await. Whether in career, academics, or any competitive field, this fortune promises that your efforts will place you at the very top.

### VIETNAMESE TRANSLATION

Thượng Quẻ (Tốt / Cát)

**Bài Thơ Quẻ:**
Trời cao vòi vọi một cành cao,
Phân minh đoán định gió chín thu.
Thừa thời dũng cảm biết vô địch,
Điện thí danh vang đệ nhất công.

**Giải Quẻ:**
Nếu cầu danh lợi, sẽ chiếm ngôi đầu. Trăng sáng trên trời, vạn dặm gặp thu.

**Ý Nghĩa Mở Rộng:**
• Đây là quẻ cực kỳ tốt lành, dự báo thành công đứng đầu trong các cuộc cạnh tranh. Như đỗ đầu khoa thi, bạn sẽ nổi bật là người thắng cuộc rõ ràng trong bất cứ điều gì bạn theo đuổi.
• Thời điểm hoàn hảo cho hành động táo bạo. Hình ảnh "thừa thời" gợi ý cơ hội đã chín muồi và sự dũng cảm của bạn sẽ được đền đáp với thành công vô song.
• Danh tiếng và sự công nhận đang chờ đợi. Dù trong sự nghiệp, học vấn, hay bất kỳ lĩnh vực cạnh tranh nào, quẻ này hứa hẹn nỗ lực của bạn sẽ đưa bạn lên vị trí cao nhất.

### ENGLISH TRANSLATION

Lower Fortune (Caution Advised)

**Fortune Poem:**
The Dark Warrior descends bringing financial loss,
Further guard against thieves and unexpected disasters.
Among these there is one who knows of heaven,
Coming from the shadows, none can resist.

**Interpretation:**
The Dark Warrior in conflict, harmony does not align. Due to resentment, no words spoken, inviting further disasters.

**Extended Interpretation:**
• This fortune warns of financial losses caused by hidden or shadowy influences. The Dark Warrior (Xuanwu) represents secret enemies, thieves, and deception—be extremely vigilant about your possessions and transactions.
• Silent grudges and unspoken resentments are creating problems. Address conflicts directly rather than letting them fester, as suppressed anger is attracting additional misfortune.
• Take practical security measures: protect valuables, be cautious with financial dealings, and be wary of people who seem friendly but may harbor ulterior motives.

• Patience is required as you wait for recognition. Do not despair at your current humble position; the right person or opportunity will reveal your exceptional qualities in due time.

### ENGLISH TRANSLATION

Lower Fortune (Caution Advised)

**Fortune Poem:**
The White Tiger at the gate, matters are inauspicious,
It can bring unexpected disasters and calamities.
Further guard against lawsuits and prison worries,
If not official harm, then it is blood injury.

**Interpretation:**
The White Tiger at the gate, all matters are inauspicious. Much unexpected disaster is invited; illness and official harm await.

**Extended Interpretation:**
• This is a serious warning fortune. The White Tiger is one of the most feared symbols in Chinese astrology, representing violence, accidents, legal troubles, and bloodshed when appearing at one's "gate."
• Physical danger is indicated—take extra care to avoid accidents, injuries, and confrontations. This is not a time to take physical risks or engage in dangerous activities.
• Legal matters and conflicts with authorities are especially perilous now. Avoid any actions that could lead to official trouble, lawsuits, or run-ins with the law.
